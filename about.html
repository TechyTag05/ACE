ACE Global Industrial Solutions (AGIS)

ACE Global Industrial Solutions (AGIS) is a trusted organization specializing in corrosion protection, surface preparation, protective coatings, inspection, and industrial maintenance services.

With a steadfast commitment to quality, innovation, and reliability, AGIS delivers end-to-end solutions that meet the most demanding global standards — ensuring long-term asset protection, operational efficiency, and superior performance.

Our expertise spans across diverse industries including Oil & Gas, Marine, Petrochemical, Power, and Infrastructure sectors. We provide a comprehensive portfolio of services ranging from third-party inspection, abrasive blasting, coating applications, rope access, scaffolding, waterproofing, epoxy flooring, and corrosion management consultation, to technical training and equipment supply.

At the core of our operations is a team of internationally certified professionals (AMPP, NACE, SSPC, BGAS, and FROSIO) who ensure technical precision, uncompromising safety, and unmatched quality in every project. Whether it’s mitigating corrosion on offshore structures, executing coating projects for refineries, or training the next generation of coating inspectors — AGIS stands as a trusted partner in industrial excellence.

Our Expertise

AGIS delivers integrated solutions across the asset life cycle, helping clients reduce risk, extend service life, and maintain compliance with international standards.

Our core areas of expertise include:

  • Third-Party Inspection & Quality Assurance

    Independent inspection services to ensure materials, surface preparation, coating application, and finished systems meet specified standards and project requirements.

  • Protective Coatings & Surface Preparation

    Comprehensive coating solutions designed to protect assets from corrosion, chemical exposure, and harsh environmental conditions. Our services cover surface preparation, coating application, inspection, and documentation.

  • Corrosion Audits & Asset Integrity Support

    Detailed corrosion assessments, reporting, and control strategies to support informed maintenance decisions and prevent premature asset failure.

  • Technical Training & Skill Development

    Practical, industry-oriented training programs for inspectors, coating professionals, and technicians—focused on standards, best practices, and site execution..

Our Approach

At AGIS, we believe effective industrial solutions come from technical clarity, disciplined execution, and ethical practices.

  • Standards-Focused Execution

    Our services are aligned with globally recognized standards such as SSPC/AMPP, ISO, and project-specific specifications.

  • Field-Driven Solutions

    We understand site realities and deliver solutions that are practical, achievable, and sustainable—not just theoretical.

  • Quality & Documentation

    Every project is supported by clear inspection records, reports, and traceability to ensure confidence and audit readiness.

  • Client-Centric Mindset

    We work closely with clients to understand their operational challenges and tailor solutions that fit their technical and commercial needs.
