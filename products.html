Industrial Paints & Protective Coating Systems

AGIS is engaged in the sales and supply of industrial paints and protective coating systems suitable for corrosion protection and asset durability across multiple industries. Our portfolio includes products from reputed manufacturers such as Asian Paints, Sigma Paints (PPG), Berger Paints, along with other internationally approved brands.

Coating Testing & Inspection Instruments

AGIS supplies a wide range of coating testing and inspection instruments used for quality control and verification during surface preparation and coating application. Our offerings include instruments from established brands such as Elcometer, DeFelsko, and other market-available brands meeting industry requirements.

Blasting & Painting Equipment

AGIS provides blasting and painting equipment required for effective surface preparation and coating application. Our supply range includes blasting pots, airless spray machines, cup guns, paint spray nozzles, tips, and other essential accessories supporting safe and efficient site execution.

Coating Chemicals & Consumables

(Common name for Thinners, Solvents & Cleaning Agents) AGIS supplies coating chemicals and consumables, including thinners, solvents, and cleaning agents used for surface cleaning, equipment maintenance, and coating application support. These products are selected to ensure compatibility with coating systems and compliance with project specifications.
