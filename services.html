Third-Party Inspection Services

ACE Global Industrial Solutions (AGIS) provides independent third-party inspection services to verify compliance with project specifications, applicable standards, and quality requirements across industrial projects.

Our inspection activities are carried out in accordance with AMPP/SSPC standards, ISO requirements, and client-approved Inspection & Test Plans (ITPs). Services are supported by structured reporting and traceable documentation.

Service Scope Includes:

  • Surface preparation inspection
  • Coating application inspection
  • Dry Film Thickness (DFT), adhesion, and holiday testing
  • Final acceptance inspection and reporting

Client Value: Objective quality verification, reduced rework, and assured compliance.

Technical Training Services

AGIS delivers industry-oriented technical training programs designed to enhance competency, standards awareness, and practical site execution skills. Our training programs include preparatory classes for AMPP, NACE, and BGAS, along with hands-on skill development courses.

Training Programs Include:

  • AMPP / NACE / BGAS preparatory classes
  • Blaster & Painter training
  • Site Supervisor training
  • QC tools familiarization training
  • Coating inspection and surface preparation awareness

Client Value: Improved workforce competency, reduced errors, and better standards compliance.

Painting & Coating Projects

AGIS undertakes on-site blasting and painting projects for industrial assets, structures, and facilities. Our project execution focuses on safety, quality, and durability.

We also execute waterproofing coating applications for industrial and commercial structures, ensuring proper surface preparation and system compatibility.

Service Scope Includes:

  • On-site abrasive blasting
  • Protective coating application
  • Waterproofing coating systems
  • Inspection and quality control during execution

Client Value: Durable coating performance, controlled execution, and extended asset service life.

Scaffolding Services

AGIS provides scaffolding services to support safe access for inspection, maintenance, blasting, painting, and repair activities.

All scaffolding solutions are designed and erected with a strong focus on safety, stability, and site-specific requirements, supporting efficient project execution.

Service Scope Includes:

  • Erection and dismantling of scaffolding
  • Access solutions for industrial structures
  • Support for maintenance and coating works

Client Value: Safe access, improved productivity, and reduced operational risk.

Rope Access Services

AGIS offers rope access services for inspection, maintenance, and coating activities in difficult-to-reach or confined areas.

Rope access methods minimize the need for heavy access equipment while maintaining safety and efficiency.

Service Scope Includes:

  • Inspection support at height
  • Maintenance and coating access
  • Limited-space and complex-access solutions

Client Value: Cost-effective access, reduced downtime, and enhanced safety.

Corrosion Audit & Technical Consultation

AGIS provides corrosion audits and technical consultation services to assess asset condition, identify degradation mechanisms, and recommend corrective actions. Our assessments support informed maintenance planning and long-term asset integrity strategies.

Service Scope Includes:

  • Visual condition assessment
  • Corrosion risk identification
  • Technical recommendations and reporting

Client Value: Early detection of corrosion risks, optimized maintenance planning, and improved asset reliability.
